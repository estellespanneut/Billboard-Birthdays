About This App


Hello! This is Shana and Estelle, two Georgetown students in the OPIM-244 class.

The purpose of this Billboard on Your Birthday app is to find the list of songs that topped your chart of choice on your (or a friend's) birthday.

To use this app, enter the desired birthdate and chart into the birthday form located in the top right of this page.

Thank you for using our app, and we hope you enjoy!
